Frequently Asked Questions


What is RealWorks?

We are a full-service real estate brokerage representing buyers and sellers in their residential real estate transactions.

How is RealWorks similar to other residential real estate brokerages?

We are regulated and licensed by state agencies to protect consumer interests. We provide service on par or better than most other brokerages. We are members of the Local Board of Realtors, the Southland Regional Association of Realtors and adhere to the organizations' strict Code of Ethics.

How is RealWorks different from other residential real estate brokerages?

We give home buyers a rebate check at closing: Usually 1.25% of the purchase price.

How can you afford to provide your services at such a discount?

Because of our rebate program, RealWorks does a higher volume of transactions. Making less money per transaction is more than offset by the increase in the number of transactions we do. Also, we harness the power of the Internet to streamline the work, saving you and us time and money.

Do I have to own a computer or have access to the Internet?

No, not at all. We give you the best of both worlds - real people and technology. It's always your choice.

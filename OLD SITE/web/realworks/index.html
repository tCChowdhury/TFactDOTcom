In the meantime, feel free to read some more facts regarding Sherman Oaks:

The Sherman Oaks Real Estate Market in one of the most prominent in the San Fernando Valley, a suburb of Los Angeles . It has an average home price of $795,000. Single family homes here range from $649,000 to a very pricy $3,500,000. Other housing types available include investment properties, town-homes & condominiums. 

Wonderful Sherman Oaks is known for its temperate climate and all it has to offer. Whether it is fine dining, recreation, shops or simply a nice café to relax in, you'll be sure to find it in Sherman Oaks. The average temperature for summer ranges from 87 in the day to 55 at night. Sherman Oaks was incorporated in 1948 and is set against the foothills of the Santa Monica Mountains . Minutes away are major film and TV production studios. Sherman Oaks is home to a wide selection of convenient commercial stores including the Sherman Oaks Galleria, and Fashion Square . In addition there are numerous historical landmarks open to the public.

Sherman Oaks has many types of living accommodations for people looking to move there; from apartment housing to condominium complexes as well as single-family residences. This area attracts many working professional from an variety of backgrounds including business owners and executives to stars of the stage and screen. Many thrive on easy access and walking distance to shops, or spend time in the local coffee houses along Ventura Boulevard .

Through the heart of Sherman Oaks run two major LA freeways – the 101 and 405. The 101 Freeway runs east and west through the valley.  The 405 Freeway connects to the 10 Freeway and will take you to downtown Los Angeles . The 405 Freeway also takes you to Long Beach South, and interchanges to Santa Monica , taking you along the Pacific Coast Highway . Sherman Oaks is a conveniently located with easy access to the greater Los Angeles area.
